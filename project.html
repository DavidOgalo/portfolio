Techsage Solutions

Visit Website

Designed and developed a website for an IT consultancy company, Techsage Solutions. The client requested for an ERP solution to manage key parts of operations, including accounting and resource management so ERPNext was used as a development platform through its website feature. The choice of using ERPNext was mainly down its efficiency in intergrating all these features that automate and manage core business processes for optimal performance. The ERP system improves Techsage in the following ways;

  • 1
    Drives optimal performance

    With solutions that use AI, Techsage will access insights that enhance their decision making and reveal ways to improve operational performance going forward.

  • 2
    Accelerates operational impact

    By connecting processes and data, Techsage will bring more visibility and flexibility to employees, helping them take action quickly and deliver more value across the business.

  • 3
    Ensures business agility

    Many ERP solutions are built to adapt to your needs and grow with you, helping you proactively prepare for—and readily respond to—any operational disruption or market change.

Techsage's vision was a beautiful and efficient website

With this in mind, I was tasked with coming up with a design that will impact how the clients percieve the company's brand and improve trust through brand consistency. Some of the features I implemented are;

  • A good SEO strategy I factored in both off-page and on-page elements that can have an impact on your site's visability to Google. My strategy mainly encompassed frequently published, good quality content.
  • Browser Consistency I ensured the website appears and behaves consistently across all major browsers such as Chrome, Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari and Opera.
  • Well-Formatted Content That Is Easy to Scan Most average internet users skim through content, scanning through key parts of the page quickly to determine if it is relevant to their needs. I formatted the content with this in mind.
  • Effective Navigation To improve the website's usability, I ensured the navigation is clutter-free.
  • Mobile Compatibility Developing a mobile optimized website is a necessity in all projects I work on.
